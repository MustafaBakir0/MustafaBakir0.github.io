About Me

I'm a multimedia designer and computer science enthusiast with a deep passion for merging creativity with cutting-edge technology. With a strong foundation in both visual storytelling and computational thinking, I thrive at the crossroads where design meets logic—where aesthetics enhance function, and algorithms empower imagination.


My experience spans a diverse range of projects, from building efficient and scalable data structures to designing immersive digital experiences that captivate and inform. This unique blend allows me to approach problems with a rare dual perspective: analytical rigor grounded in computer science, and expressive vision driven by design.


In project management, I excel at translating abstract ideas into actionable plans, bringing together interdisciplinary teams to execute with clarity and impact. I'm particularly drawn to the strategic side of design—ensuring every product or experience not only meets usability and visual standards but also contributes meaningfully to business objectives and user needs.


Outside of structured work, I'm constantly exploring the evolving relationship between art and technology. Whether it's experimenting with interactive media, prototyping creative tools, or engaging with the latest developments in AI and software design, I remain committed to lifelong learning and innovation.


If you're interested in crafting work that's thoughtful, impactful, and future-facing—I'd love to connect. Let's build something remarkable together.
