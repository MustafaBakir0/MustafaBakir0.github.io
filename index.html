Mustafa & Isaac

Redemption Through Reflection

Every choice defines who we become.

ABOUT

Mustafa holds a grudge against Isaac, and when he stumbles upon Isaac’s lost wallet, he sees an opportunity for petty revenge. But as he opens it, expecting cash, he finds nothing—except a moment of reflection. Confronted with his own wrongdoing, Mustafa realizes that stealing, even out of spite, is wrong. Swallowing his pride, he returns the wallet to Isaac, who, despite their past conflicts, thanks him. This short film explores the weight of our choices, the power of integrity, and the unexpected strength found in forgiveness.
